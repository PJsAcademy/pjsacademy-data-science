# 📊 Phase 2 — Advanced Data Science Projects

10 analytics projects that tell stories with data. These focus on EDA, visualisation, statistics, and data storytelling — with Indian and global datasets.

---

## 1. 🏏 IPL Analytics Universe
**Difficulty:** ⭐⭐⭐ | **Skills:** pandas, Plotly, statistical analysis, ball-by-ball data

Deep-dive into 15 seasons of IPL ball-by-ball data. Player impact scores, venue effects, powerplay/death-over analysis, and a "clutch factor" metric.

- **v1:** Team & player stats dashboard
- **v2:** Custom metrics — pressure index, matchup analysis (batter vs bowler)
- **v3:** Interactive win-probability model + venue-adjusted player ratings
- **Innovation:** A novel "clutch factor" combining match situation + performance
- **Resume line:** *"Analysed 200K+ IPL deliveries to build a clutch-performance metric and win-probability visualiser."*

---

## 2. 💰 Indian Stock Market Regime Detector
**Difficulty:** ⭐⭐⭐ | **Skills:** yfinance, statistics, HMM, change-point detection

Detect bull/bear/sideways market regimes for NIFTY 50 using statistical change-point detection. Overlay regimes on price charts.

- **v1:** Volatility + return analysis
- **v2:** Rolling-window regime classification
- **v3:** Hidden Markov Model regime detection + sector rotation heatmap
- **Innovation:** Unsupervised regime discovery with HMM
- **Resume line:** *"Built a market-regime detector for NIFTY 50 using Hidden Markov Models and change-point analysis."*

## 3. 🗺️ India Census Story Map
**Difficulty:** ⭐⭐ | **Skills:** GeoPandas, choropleth maps, demographic analysis

Interactive maps of India's census data — literacy, sex ratio, urbanisation, income — down to district level, with correlation storytelling.

- **v1:** State-level choropleth maps
- **v2:** District-level + bivariate maps
- **v3:** Animated time-series (2001 vs 2011 vs projections) + insight generator
- **Innovation:** Bivariate choropleths (2 variables, 1 map)
- **Resume line:** *"Created district-level bivariate choropleth maps revealing literacy-income correlations across India."*

## 5. 🌦️ Climate Change Dashboard (India Focus)
**Difficulty:** ⭐⭐ | **Skills:** time series, anomaly detection, Plotly, trend analysis

Visualise 100+ years of Indian temperature/rainfall data. Detect warming trends, monsoon shifts, and extreme-event frequency.

- **v1:** Temperature trend charts
- **v2:** Rainfall anomaly + monsoon onset shift analysis
- **v3:** Extreme-event frequency + region comparison + forecast band
